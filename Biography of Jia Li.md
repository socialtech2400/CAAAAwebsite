李佳（Vivian Li），中美双执业律师，长期从事跨境商事、房地产及争议解决法律事务。
现任上海博芸律师事务所合伙人，同时为美国 LightBar LLC 联合创始人及法律资格考试讲师，致力于法律教育与专业人才培养。
此前，李佳曾长期担任财富五百强地产企业世邦魏理仕（CBRE）中国区法务顾问，深度参与多个地标性房地产项目交易、跨境并购及重大商事争议处理，包括涉案金额巨大的最高人民法院级别诉讼及国际仲裁案件。
李佳长期关注华裔法律群体的发展，认同本协会的宗旨与使命，荣幸作为 Founding Member 参与并支持协会的成立与发展。

Jia (Vivian) Li is an attorney licensed to practice in California and China, with extensive experience in cross-border commercial, real estate, and dispute resolution matters.
She currently serves as a Partner at Shanghai Boyun Law Firm and is also a Co-Founder and Bar Tutor at LightBar LLC, a U.S.-based legal education platform dedicated to legal training and professional development.
Previously, Vivian served as legal counsel for CBRE China, a Fortune 500 global real estate company, where she was deeply involved in landmark real estate transactions, cross-border mergers and acquisitions, and complex commercial disputes, including high-value litigation before China’s Supreme People’s Court and international arbitration proceedings.
Vivian is committed to supporting the growth and empowerment of the Chinese American legal community and is honored to serve as a Founding Member in support of the Association’s mission.
